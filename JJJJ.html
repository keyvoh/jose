About Speed Demons Racing

Our History

Founded in 2010, Speed Demons Racing began as a small team of passionate racers in Detroit. Over the years, we've grown into one of the most respected names in amateur and professional racing circuits.

Our Mission

To push the boundaries of speed and performance while maintaining the highest safety standards. We believe racing should be accessible to all who share our passion.

Our Team

We have a dedicated team of 15 professionals including mechanics, engineers, and of course, our world-class drivers who compete in events worldwide.
